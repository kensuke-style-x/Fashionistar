Privacy Policy

Personal information

Seven Star will not use a user's personal information in any way. A user who share photos is required to sign in, but Seven Star does not use any personal information.

Location information

A subscription user's location will be needed to get the user's weather information、but Seven Star will not get the user's location information in any way.
